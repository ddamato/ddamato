# Design Systems Architect

- Founder & Chief Architect of [Design Systems House](https://ds.house); a consultancy dedicated to the future of design systems.
- Author of [Mise en Mode](https://mode.place) which demonstrates how we might execute design decisions not through complex token naming exercises, but by burdening rectangles with glorious purpose.
- Adjunct faculty at [Parsons School of Design](https://www.newschool.edu/parsons/) and the [Fashion Institute of Technology](https://www.fitnyc.edu/).
- Currently, the engineering lead for the UXCore library at [GoDaddy](https://www.godaddy.com/)
- Previously, the first UX Engineer at [Compass](https://www.compass.com/).
